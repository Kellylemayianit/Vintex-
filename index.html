The Kimana Experience

A town built by the road
to Amboseli, not around it.

Kimana is a working town — irrigation canals, a market on trading days, and a wildlife corridor that still moves elephants between Amboseli and Chyulu after dark. Vintex was built into that rhythm rather than against it: early breakfasts before game drives, quiet rooms for the return, and a front desk that keeps local hours.

We keep the house plain on purpose. What you're here for is outside the window.
